## ✨ Project Overview

📊 Developed an end-to-end HR Analytics and Workforce Intelligence solution using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

👨‍💼 Analyzed workforce data containing **2,700+ employee records** to uncover insights related to employee promotions, retirement planning, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and organizational performance.

📈 Built an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables HR teams to monitor workforce health, identify promotion opportunities, and improve succession planning.

🚀 From raw workforce data → business insights → executive decision support dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

* 📌 Monitor workforce demographics and employee distribution
* 📈 Identify employees due for promotion
* 👥 Analyze retirement readiness across departments
* 😊 Evaluate employee satisfaction and engagement
* ⚠️ Support workforce planning and succession management
* 💡 Enable data-driven HR decision-making

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Promotion Planning

* 72.73% of employees are identified as due for promotion.
* Several departments show high promotion demand requiring workforce planning.

### Retirement Planning

* 723 employees are approaching retirement eligibility.
* Succession planning is required to minimize operational risk.

### Workforce Demographics

* Male employees account for 66.86% of the workforce.
* Female employees account for 33.14% of the workforce.

### Employee Satisfaction

* Job satisfaction levels vary significantly across workforce groups.
* Low-satisfaction segments require focused HR intervention.

### Workforce Intelligence

* Service year distribution highlights experienced workforce concentration.
* Job level analysis supports talent management and career progression planning.
